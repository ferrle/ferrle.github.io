About

What We Do

At Ferrle, we love simplicity. Our product helps to simplify the home furnishings process by providing users with a tool to automate the measuring of spaces, interior design, and online buying process. We bring you directly to the suppliers and provide you with the ultimate furniture buying experience.

Our History

Lincoln and Ralf developed the idea for Ferrle as roommates at USC. Lincoln had been exploring ideas for the furniture industry and pitched a consumer facing 3-D interior design app to Ralf. Lincoln then spent months creating and refining a business concept and plan. From there, the quest to launch Ferrle began.

Meet the Team

Meet Lincoln Yeung. He began his career working in his father’s art gallery as a secretary, salesperson, and gallery aide. After spending 6 years in the industry, he moved on and earned two degrees from USC: a B.S. in Accounting and a B.S. in Business Administration. Lincoln is our team’s jack of all trades. He fills in for every possible non-technical role to give our technical team the best possible support.

Meet Ralf Cheung. He studies at USC and is finishing up his M.S. Computer Science degree after completing a bachelor’s in the same field. Ralf is our resident tech guy, and he’s able to pump out code like a machine.
